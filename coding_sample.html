      Simulating a complex system

      Complex system were introduce as a mathematical curiosity, that later proves to be behind different procces in physics, biology and social behaviour.
      When dealing with complex system we aren't able to arrive to a closed form solution. Therefore, questions like: does the porcess converges to an sationaty equilibrium? When it does? How are the equilibrium properties? require simulations
      The code presented uses MATLAB for making those simulations.
      The simulations model voting patterns. Voters are placed in a 2-dimension matrix, each voter has to choose among three parties, voters are heterogeneous in their preferences, every voter have a prefered party, a 'not so bad' party, an a 'hated' party. Before voting, voters can made small polls among their neighbourhood, which are used for estimating the probability of each party for winning. In summary, the neighbourhood infuence the expectations of each households. If a household expects that her favourite party would be last, she then changes her voting intention. That is, is she would have to vote, she would vote her second best option.
      Up to here, the model itself it is not 'complex'. But afeter every voter actualized its voting intention they can conduct a new survey, different voting intentions leads to different voting expectation, that again leads to different voting intentions.
