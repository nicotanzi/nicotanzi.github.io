Economic and Financial Consulting Expirience

Kevin D. Oden & Associates

Currently, I am Model Validator at KDOA. I scrutinize models with great rigor, perform backtesting to evaluate their accuracy, and compare them against benchmark models that I develop. Additionally, I conduct sensitivity analyses to stress-test the models and observe their responses. Each validation concludes with a comprehensive document that describes the model, presents the analyses, and highlights the issues found. This process involves report writing, effective communication of findings, and openess to feedback from model owners.

Beex

2018. Risk Modeler.
I developed a risk model for Banco Hipotecario in SAS programming.
The model loads the bank's portfolio and, for each user-customized scenario, projects cash flows for every asset and liability according to the scenario parameters. These cash flows are then discounted using an interest rate curve associated with the scenario. This model allows for the measurement of the bank portfolio's value under specific scenarios. Additionally, the variability among scenarios allows to estimate the risk of the portfolio.
