Additionally, I conduct sensitivity analysis to stress-test the model and observe how it responds. Finally, every validation ends with a document describing the model, presenting the analysis, and raising the issues found, which envolves report writing, smoothness to comunicate the findings as well as openess to hear the model owner's feed back.

Beex

2018. Risk Modeler.
I developed a risk model for the Banco Hipotecario in SAS language programming.
Briefly, the model loads the bank portfolio, then for every user-customized scenario, it calculate a cashflow for every asset and liability acording to the scenario parametrization. Finally, it discounts every cashflow according to an interest rate curve associated to the cashflow scenario.
It allows to meseaure the value of the bank portfolio subject to an specific scenario. The diferences in the value bettwen each scenario and the likelihood of them give a measurment of risk.
